About Us

M&R Technoservice 22 LTD was established in 2025 in Aberdeen, Scotland, United Kingdom. The company provides professional CCTV security systems, IT infrastructure solutions, and integrated technology systems for restaurants and commercial premises.

We focus on reliability, security, and efficiency, helping businesses protect their assets, improve operational control, and comply with modern security requirements.

Our Solutions

  • CCTV & Surveillance Systems – IP and analog cameras, system design, installation, and maintenance.
  • IT Infrastructure – Structured cabling, routers, switches, secure networks, and system integration.
  • Restaurant Systems – POS systems, kitchen display systems (KDS), order and payment solutions.
  • Access Control – Door access, intercom systems, and attendance control.
  • Remote Monitoring – Secure remote access and cloud-based monitoring solutions.

Services

  • Site survey and technical assessment
  • System design and professional installation
  • Configuration, testing, and commissioning
  • System upgrades and modernization
  • Maintenance contracts and emergency support
  • Technical consultation and custom solutions
