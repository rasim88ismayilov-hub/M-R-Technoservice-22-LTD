About Us

M&R Technoservice 22 LTD, established in 2025, is a leading provider of comprehensive IT, security, and restaurant management solutions. We specialize in CCTV, access control, alarm systems, face recognition, barrier gates, smart home automation, POS thermal papers, and restaurant management systems.

Our certified professional team ensures every product and service meets the highest quality standards. From installation to maintenance, we provide reliable, efficient, and secure solutions for homes and businesses. Remote monitoring and 24/7 online support are available for all clients.
